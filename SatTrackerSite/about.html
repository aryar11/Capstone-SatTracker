Using SatTracker

The SatTracker satellite visualization tool offers a number of features created specifically for satellite enthusiasts. To access the tool, click the "Tracker" button located at the top of the site. Key features of the tool include the "Nearest Satellite Finder" located on the right side of the screen, as well as the "Satellite Searcher" on the left side of the screen.


Nearest Satellite Finder

To find satellites near you, simply click "Get My Location" followed by "Find Closest Satellite". The satellites nearest to you will be highlighted in Green, and you can view their data by toggling the table on the right side of the screen.


Satellite Searcher

Simply enter the name of any satellite you wish to view, and click on it's name in the drop down list to have it highlighted on the globe, and its information appear in the table on the left.


Selected Satellite

This table displays information of the current satellite selected. Clicking the "More Info" button perfroms a google search of the selected satellite.
